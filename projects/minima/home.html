FEATURES

What Minima can do for you

1. Search and discover new music

Discover the music you love with ease using our search feature! Whether you're looking for the latest hits or timeless classics, our search function lets you find your favorite songs and artists in an instant. Simply type in your query and tap the search button for immediate results!

2. Manage your playlists

Create your perfect soundtrack with our customisable playlist feature! Whether you're planning a party, working out, or just relaxing, adding and deleting tracks from a playlist is as simple as two quick taps! Want to rename your playlist? Simply tap and hold your playlist and tap "Rename"!

3. Shuffle your music

Keep your listening experience fresh and exciting with our shuffle feature! Perfect for those who love surprises, the shuffle function randomly mixes up the tracks in your playlist or library, ensuring you never know what song is coming next. Just hit the shuffle button and let our app create a dynamic and unpredictable music journey for you.

4. Control your music playback

Experience ultimate control over your listening with our sleek and intuitive music player! Equipped with all the essential features, our player allows you to play, pause, fast-forward/rewind, and skip tracks effortlessly. The player also displays album art, song titles, and artist names, ensuring you always know what's playing.

5. View and organise your queue

Take control of your listening experience with our dynamic queue feature! Easily add, remove, and rearrange songs in your queue to customize your upcoming play order on the fly. The queue provides a clear view of what's next, allowing you to anticipate and adjust your playlist without interrupting the music.

SUPPORT

Need help? Look no further

General Information

How do I sign up for the app?

Download the app and click on "Sign Up." Follow the prompts to create your account.

What platforms is the app available on?

The app is available on exclusively on Android. Download it via Google Play using the Get Started button or the Google Play badge at the top of the page.

Technical Issues

Why won't the app play music?

Ensure you have a stable internet connection and that your app is up-to-date. If the problem persists, try restarting the app.

How do I download songs for offline listening?

Downloading songs is not yet currently supported by our app. Rest assured, this is a feature our software development team is working on!

Features and Functionality

How do I create a playlist?

Tap the "Playlists" button on the navigation bar. Then, tap the "Plus" button to create a new playlist.

Does this app have "auto-play"?

No, this is one of the key data-saving features of this app. You must specify the songs you'd like to listen to as this avoids unnecessary loading of audio which might not be listened to.

Privacy and Security

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to protect your data. Check our Privacy Policy for more details.

How can I view the data you've collected about me?

You can't! We do not collect any data in regards to our users. The only data stored is your playlists, which we have no access to as this data is stored locally on your device.
