Our Story

Mintaka Solutions was founded in 2021 by two software developers who wanted to create a product that would help people find and enjoy music more easily. They believed that music should be accessible and enjoyable, and so they created Minima, a free, simple, and intuitive music player that helps users discover and enjoy new songs.

Since launching, Minima has grown significantly, and we've had over 50,000 downloads on the Google Play Store. We're constantly working on improving Minima to ensure it remains lightweight, fast, and user-friendly. Our team is dedicated to providing regular updates that introduce new features and refine existing ones, all while keeping data usage to a minimum.

Join us on our journey as we continue to evolve and enhance Minima, bringing the joy of music to everyone, everywhere.
